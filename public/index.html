How Distrodle Works

Find the hidden Linux distro by comparing attributes from each guess.

  • Type a distro name and click Guess.
  • Green means exact match, yellow means close, red means different.
  • For year, arrows show if the target is newer or older.
  • Every 5 misses, you get one extra hint.
  • Pool options let you include Very Low popularity and discontinued distros.
